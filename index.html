About

A notebook for light, wind, and wonder

Keeper of the Winds is a personal collection of stories where photographs come first and words arrive quietly behind them. It moves between travel, science, and the moments in between — a place to pause, to notice, and to remember how the world looks when you really see it.

What to expect:
Slow essays, generous images, and a pace closer to weather than a news feed. No pop‑ups, no noise — just stories captured through light.
